### 🎯 Strategic Overview

As an **Industrial Civil Engineer**, I identified systemic inefficiencies in the logistics cycle of critical chemical inputs (FUEL). **LOG-STRAT-001** was developed to bridge the gap between raw arrival data and strategic management control, utilizing advanced statistical modeling to act as a predictive operational hub.

### 1. The Challenge (Friction Analysis)

* **Problem:** High variability in truck "Stay and Departure" times within the lumberyard and reception areas.
* **Impact:** Operational bottlenecks, increased "hidden costs" in yard management, and potential risks to the primary production line stability.

### 2. The Purpose (Strategic Objective)

* **Standardization:** Transition from reactive monitoring to a data-driven governance framework.
* **Accuracy:** Utilize probability distribution fitting (Gamma) to ensure high-fidelity simulation models, aligning with **Kaizen** principles of continuous improvement.
* **Scalability:** Design a modular analytical framework adaptable to other high-volume industrial supply chains or BPO logistics.

### 3. Key Results (Impact Metrics)

* **Efficiency:** Identified that 50% of the transport fleet accounted for 80% of the total monthly rotation, enabling targeted fleet management.
* **Traceability:** Implementation of 5 critical KPIs for real-time monitoring of logistics performance and truck cycle control.
* **Optimization:** Deployment of a simulation model to evaluate "What-if" scenarios, successfully reducing supply chain downtime and bottlenecks.

---

### 🛠️ Technical Architecture

* **Analytical Engine:** Python & R for outlier detection, data normalization, and Gamma distribution fitting.
* **Simulation Logic:** Discrete-event simulation (Simio) to model arrival patterns and service times.
* **Visualization:** Strategic Dashboard (Excel/VBA) for real-time KPI tracking and management reporting.
