### 🎯 Strategic Overview
Applied a rigorous industrial engineering approach to mitigate a $79,233 USD annual loss caused by raw material variability. This project transitioned supplier selection from price-based to data-driven quality metrics.

### 1. The Challenge (Friction Analysis)
* **Problem:** Inconsistent rubber base quality led to temperature deviations.
* **Impact:** High batch rejection rates and significant financial leakage.

### 2. The Purpose (Strategic Objective)
* **Diagnosis:** Use t-student testing to isolate supplier performance variance.
* **Optimization:** Develop an OLS Regression model to calculate exact catalyst dosages.
* **ROI:** Secure a 2.1% total throughput increase by standardizing the supply chain.

### 3. Key Results (Impact Metrics)
* **Supplier Selection:** Statistically identified Supplier B as the only technically viable partner.
* **Precision:** Achieved a predictive model targeting the 140°C meta-temperature.
* **Financial Logic:** Justified vendor consolidation through ROI-based quality modeling.
